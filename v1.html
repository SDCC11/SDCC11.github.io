The Home Finder application uses frequented locations and desiered travel times to generate an ideal home location. You can use this application by following bellow

1) Enter a frequently visted location in the top left box
2) Enter your desiered maximum travel time to reach that location
3)Add this to your prefence list by clicking the add button

To see your inputed locations click the V button
To erase an entry, click the x next to the distance
